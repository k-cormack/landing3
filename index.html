How I Got HERE

Some folks have a few chapters in their life, and after closing a few of my own, I found myself (once again) asking "what do I want to do when I grow up?". Throughout my life, I have been intrigued with technology and gadgets, and was one of those kids who frequently took my toys apart to figure out how they work - which I still do to this day (only my toys have become more complex....and expensive!). With support and encouragement from my family, I have decided to pursue a career as a Web Developer, ready to continue my learning, tinkering, and problem-solving!
